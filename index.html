About

Freedom TLV is a sailing club which is obligated to the best service, and best vessels! we will give you the ultimate sailing experience, with a proffessional skipper, high quallity sound system, big tube, white wine and more.

Our fleet is based in the Frenchtown Harbor Marina, a small, modern marina that also hosts private and visiting yachts. Marina facilities include clean rest rooms with hot and cold water and showers, an ice machine, neighborhood laundromat, convenience store, post office and bank a short walk away.
